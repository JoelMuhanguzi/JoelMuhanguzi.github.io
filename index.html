Research Interests

What I Work On

Urban Noise & Bioacoustics

Deploying edge ML models for urban noise classification and wildlife bioacoustic sensing in constrained environments.

Environmental Sensing

Air quality monitoring and biodiversity conservation through solar-powered IoT nodes and on-device inference.

Efficient Edge AI

Model quantization, pruning, and TinyML optimization for deployment on memory- and power-constrained hardware.

African Language NLP

Low-resource multilingual NLP for African languages — translation pipelines, LLM finetuning, synthetic data generation.

Citizen Feedback Systems

AI-driven voice feedback devices for assessing public service quality in local government and public institutions.

IoT Hardware Design

PCB design, solar energy harvesting circuits, and firmware development for long-running remote sensing deployments.
